QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

What is a "hydroponic" system?

A hydroponic system is a method of growing plants without soil, using nutrient-rich water to deliver everything they need for healthy growth.

Why choose hydroponics?

Hydroponics uses significantly less water than traditional farming, grows plants faster, and produces fresh food in a clean, controlled environment.

Is hydroponics beginner-friendly?

Yes. Modern hydroponic systems are easy to use and require minimal gardening experience, making them ideal for homes, schools, and small communities.
